## Reporting a Vulnerability

Use this section to tell people how to report a vulnerability.

Please report sensitive security issues via email to [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS].

## Process

1.  **Report**: Send a detailed description of the vulnerability to the email above.
2.  **Ack**: We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours.
3.  **Review**: We will investigate the issue and determine its impact.
4.  **Fix**: If confirmed, we will work on a patch.
5.  **Disclosure**: Once patched, we will coordinate public disclosure.

We appreciate your effort to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.
